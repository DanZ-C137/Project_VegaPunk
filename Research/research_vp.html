Work History:


At some point, Vegapunk worked alongside Vinsmoke Judge within MADS, an illegal research team, and discovered the blueprint of life known as the Lineage Factor. When the World Government caught wind of this, they deemed the research a threat, and Vegapunk was arrested. Despite this run in with the bureaucracy, he was employed as their chief scientist in the Marines and has played a pivotal role in strengthening the power of the Navy.
